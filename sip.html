Student Innovation Project

SIP

Project Description

  • As the user interacts more on its mental health issues, the chatbot will learn from its interaction and consult and provide psychological support to the user.
  • Act as the first line of defense for the user's mental health.
  • Store user data and record it under a journal entry so the user can look back.
  • Motivate users to engage in mental health exercises.
  • WARNING: Not intended to replace the user's medication and professional health advisors.

Innovation Claim
Create a therapy chatbot that can…

  • Check on user’s mental health.
  • Personalize itself depending on user interaction.
  • Collect data and store it under a journal entry section.
  • Detect red flags and persuade user to call a support line.
  • Motivate users to engage in mental health exercises.
